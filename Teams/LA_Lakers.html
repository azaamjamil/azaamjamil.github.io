Los Angeles Lakers



The Los Angeles Lakers are based out of Los Angeles, California. They play at Staples Center in an arena shared by the Los Angeles Clippers. The Los Angeles Lakers are one of the most successful teams in the history of the NBA, and have won 16 championships over the years. An estimate in 2015 places the Lakers as the second most valuable NBA franchise, with a value estimated to be near $2.7 billion. The duo of Shaquille O Neal and Kobe Bryant shook the NBA in the first decade of the 21st century, going for three championships under the leadership of Phil Jackson. The purple and yellow jersey is one that is recognized in all corners of the world, as the epitome of basketball greatness.
