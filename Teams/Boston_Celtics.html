Boston Celtics



The Boston Celtics currently hold the most championships out of all the teams in the NBA, one more than the Los Angeles Lakers. They've had their fair share of superstars, with Larry Bird, Bill Russell, and more recently the rein of Paul Pierce, Kevin Garnett, and Ray Allen. The Lakers-Celtics rivalry was one for the ages, as they met a record 12 times in the finals. The Celtics, similar to their old time rivals, are currently in the rebuilding process after letting go of the big three a couple years ago.
