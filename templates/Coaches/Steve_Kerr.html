Steve Kerr




Joined Joined the Golden State Warriors as head coach in 2015. In his rookie year, he led Klay Thompson and Stephen Curry to their first championship. During the 2016 off-season, the Warriors acquired Kevin Durant, which created an even stronger team.

Interesting fact: Kerr was the first rookie coach to ever win a championship as the Golden State Warriors went in 2015. It was also under Kerr that the team set the record for most wins in a regular season.
